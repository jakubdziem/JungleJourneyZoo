About Jungle Journey Zoo

Established in 1995 by a group of dedicated primatologists, Monkey City has been home to a diverse array of monkey species from the lush rainforests of South America to the vibrant jungles of Africa. We are committed to exploring the world, discovering unique primates, and providing them a sanctuary here in our zoo.

We promise that you will be captivated by our playful residents from the moment you step through our gates until the time you leave. Join us for a family day out, a fun excursion with friends, or simply to enjoy some peaceful time surrounded by the wonders of nature.
